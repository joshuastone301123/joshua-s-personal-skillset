Every weekly NYC dance-music discovery briefing must include a **💬 Feedback** section at the bottom of the page, structured as one block with prompts for each subsection:

- **🎤 Artists:**
- **🎵 Songs:**
- **🏷️ Labels:**
- **🎪 Party Series:**
- **Overall vibe / anything to do more or less of next week:**

Place it after the final compiled-date italic line, separated by a `---`.

**Why:** Josh wants to leave targeted feedback per subsection so future briefings drift toward what lands and away from what misses. Single block (not inline per pick) is his chosen shape — tidier than per-item prompts.

**How to apply:**
- Include this Feedback block in every new weekly discovery page going forward (the Notion page still carries the section as a backup/archive, even though Josh prefers Telegram for the actual feedback flow).
- **Feedback channel: Telegram only.** Josh sends all feedback through the Dance Music Discovery Telegram bot — not in this chat, not in the Notion page. Do NOT prompt for feedback in chat after compiling a briefing; the Telegram teaser already invites a reply. Wait for it to arrive via Telegram.
- When Josh sends feedback through Telegram, save it **immediately** as a feedback memory — don't wait until next week's compile. Each note should capture what was praised or rejected and *why*, so future picks can be calibrated. Use names like `feedback_weekly_discovery_<topic>.md` and link related memories with `[[...]]`.
- Before assembling each new briefing, scan memory for any feedback memories tagged to weekly discovery and let them shape selections.
